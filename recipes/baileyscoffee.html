Baileys Coffee

Description

Baileys coffee is a delicious beverage you can make at home.

Ingredients

  • Baileys
  • Coffee
  • Whipped cream
  • Chocolate sprinkles

Steps

  1. Boil coffee
  2. Mix 50% of coffee with 50% baileys irish creme
  3. Add whipped cream on top
  4. Add chocolate sprinkles on whipped cream
